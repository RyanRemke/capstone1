MadDog Tackle

In 2010, Rob started to make his own baits for muskie fishing. The Mama's have 5 inch blades and the Baby's have 4 inch blades. Both baits are especially good for early-season action.

Very effective bait in most circumstances. Comes in all kinds of colors/varieties. This bait attracts muskie while also reducing the stress on wrists when reeling in.
