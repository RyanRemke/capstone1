History

MadDog Outdoors is based upon our fish-loving dachsund, Madi. She passed away in 2012 after 12 years on the water.

MadDog Outdoors' guide is Rob Remke. He has lived in Minnesota for 25+ years. He has plenty of experience with the waters in Minnesota and Wisconsin. Our highlight destinations include:

  • Mille Lacs Lake
  • Lake Waconia
  • Bone Lake
  • Chippewa Flowage
  • Lake Minnetonka

Rob is an engineer with a skill in making baits and has always loved to take people fishing. Rob has experience with bass fishing, musky fishing, trout fishing, pan fishing, etc.

Mission

MadDog Outdoors seeks to provide quality tackle for musky fishermen accross the country. Also, we strive to provide our best services for fishermen in Minnesota and Wisconsin. Regardless of experience, be prepared to have a great time!

Our goal is to provide a nice time on the water while also maintaining a strong relationship with our customers.
