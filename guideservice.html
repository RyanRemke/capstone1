MadDog Guide Service

MadDog Outdoors offers guide services for all groups and ages.

MadDog Outdoors guides for musky, bass and pan fishing in the waters of Minnesota and Wisconsin.

Up to 2-3 people are allowed to come along on a trip. Rob uses a 2004 Alumncraft walleye boat.

Scheduling options are as follows:

  • Half-Day: $49.99
  • Full-Day: $99.99
  • Evening Trip: $29.99

Fishing Locations

Lakes: Mille Lacs, Bone, Green, Minnetonka, Waconia, Ann, Shutz, Chippewa Flowage, Alexander, etc.

Rivers: Mississippi, St. Croix, etc.

Contact Rob Remke for more location options or questions.
